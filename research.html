Research

I favor broad research interests in many deep learning and machine learning related areas.

My Ph.D. work is in machine learning and deep learning area, where I study and apply machine learning algorithms into different application domains.

I focus specifically in these topics:

  • Filter bubbles— Study the filter bubbles in political news recommender systems
  • Hate speech detection —Cyberbully activities; offensive language detection
  • Microscopy images analysis—Cell activities classification through image learning
